Digital Skills for Women+

Digital Nova Scotia - June 2024

Learning program created by women who have experienced firsthand the barriers into the technology industry. This 12-session program is designed to help women and gender diverse individuals who are interested in the tech sector and would like to learn more about industry-related topics, the ecosystem, and career opportunities. Its purpose is to help participants build confidence, learn industry language, and ultimately leave better positioned to explore and enter a career in tech.

Topics Covered:
  • Digital Marketing Strategy
  • Data Management
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Paid Advertising
  • Cybersecurity
  • Artifical Intelligence (AI)
